Fyre Rope

This long vine is fast growing, taking only 3 months to maature. They can grow up to 80-120 feet in length. The stems are shades of light and dark brown. They tangle together in thick mats with a diameter of 4 inches.

The leaves of this vine grow up to 4 inches long. They are pointed and have wavy edges. They come in a very vibrant orange which glows at night, appearing lke a fire. If enough grow in a single forest, it gives the look of a forest fire.

This vine is fairly rare. One or two might be found growing together, but there is only one forest where they grow abundantly. This forest is called the forest of flames.
